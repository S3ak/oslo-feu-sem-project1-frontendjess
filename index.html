We are Oslo's community science museum where you can discover award-winning exhibitions, special events and interactive activities/events for kids and adults of all ages. We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. That is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore.
