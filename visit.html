THE SCIENCE MUSEUM HAS NEW OPENING HOURS

Due to the pandemic, we have new opening hours. We are open on Tuesdays - Saturdays with opening hours of 10.00 - 16.00. Last entry is at 15.00.

You must book your tickets with a timeslot in advance and the maximum duration of your visit is 3 hours.

BOOK YOUR TIME SLOT

All visitors to the museum must pre-book their tickets. All children under the age of 13 must be accompanied by an adult. Children in between the ages of 13 - 17 must provide parents' or guardians' contact details when checking in.

PRICES

Admission is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

HOLIDAY CLOSE DAYS

We are closed for the holidays during December 23 - 26 and December 31 - 1.
